About Me

Hello World!

My name is Jim Nguyen and I am excited about jumping into this new world of Computer Coding. My educational background is in Psychology, but I somehow found my way into the financial industry where I have worked for the majority of my career. In this way, I see a lot of simlarities to coding. Every ingredient or element brings their own unique characteristic and when combined with other elements or ingredients - creates something better.
